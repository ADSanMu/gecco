Strategy Espresso - 27 November 2017

Where has the volatility gone? In the last two weeks, we saw volatility return only to subside again as markets recovered most of their losses. You'll also find a short recap on recent performance in sectors, smart beta, and fixed income.

Why it Makes Sense to Seek Income Through Quality European Stocks

Focusing on high yield alone may leave investors exposed during market corrections. One alternative is to seek income through quality European stocks; an established track record of dividend payment can act as a powerful quality signal.

Sector Dashboard - November 2017

Our Sector ETF Dashboard provides investors with an overview of recent sector performance and flows, and also features our Sectors to Watch for the month ahead.

Why We Like Japanese Equities

The uptrend in Japanese equities is gaining traction. Despite gains this year, Japanese equities are still less expensive than many other global equities on various valuation measures. Additionally, Abe's recent victory could signal a continuation of loose monetary policy. These are just a few of the reasons why we like Japanese equities.

Fed Continuity with Powell as New Chair

Jerome Powell has been selected to become the new Chair of the Federal Reserve. In this brief note, we share our thoughts on the selection process and what investors might be able to expect once Powell assumes his new role.

Rising Rate of Interest in US Financials

A confluence of factors has brightened the prospects for the financials sector, and investors have taken note. The sector could stand to benefit from rising bond yields as a result of improved margins, while an improving economic backdrop and potential deregulation could provide further support.

Japan Stages a Comeback on Governance Reforms

Europe - Surprising on the Upside

Returning to Europe
